A murder has occurred in the old mansion on Maple Street. The victim, Mr. Blackwood, was found dead in his study.
The police have ruled out suicide. You, the detective, must figure out who the murderer is.

Suspects:
1. Alice - The maid who worked for Mr. Blackwood for 10 years.
2. Bob - His business partner, known for financial troubles.
3. Clara - Mr. Blackwood's niece, who recently argued with him.
